TIDAL CURRENTS
In this area single day tide occurs near the time of the moon's maximum declination during the period of the spring tide and the velocity of the NE-going stream greater than 3 Knots may be encountered becuse of existence of the Tusima current(the NE-going stream with velocity of 1~1.5Knots) The stronger NE-going stream occurs in the afternoon in spring 
In the day time in summer. In the morning in autumn and at night in winter in Seo Sudo the slack water occurs later in the southwestern part than in the northeastern Part.
