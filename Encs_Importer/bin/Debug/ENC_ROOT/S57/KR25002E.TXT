TIDAL CURRENTS
In the eastward of Noryang Bridge, the E-going stream occurs approxmately from 3hrs befor HW of Yeosu Hang to 1hr after HW. When the tidal stream becoms the strongest, it is about 2.1kn in HW. The W-going stream occurs approximately from 2hrs after HW to 1hr after LW. When the tidal stream becomes the strongest, it is about 2.9kn in 1hr before LW.
