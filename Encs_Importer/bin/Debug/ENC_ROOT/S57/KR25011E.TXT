TIDAL CURRENTS
In the westward of Noryang Bridge, the E-going stream occurs approximately from 3hrs befor HW of Yeosu Hang to 2hrs after HW. When the tidal stream becomes the strogest, it is about 1.2kn in 1hr befor HW of Yosu Hang.  The w-going stream occurs approximately from 3hrs after HW to 2hrs after LW.  When the tidal stream becomes the strongest, it is about 2.1kn in LW.
